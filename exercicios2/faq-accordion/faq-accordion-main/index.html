FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me

Frontend Mentor is a platform where individuals from all skill levels can learn, grow, and build their careers as front-end developers. It helps you improve your coding skills, build projects, and land a job as a web developer.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Yes, Frontend Mentor offers free challenges, but there are also premium features available for more advanced projects.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Absolutely! All projects you complete on Frontend Mentor are yours to showcase in your portfolio. It's a great way to demonstrate your skills to potential employers or clients.

How can I get help if I am stuck on a challenge?

You can get help by joining the Frontend Mentor community on forums or social media. There are also resources and hints available to guide you through challenges.
