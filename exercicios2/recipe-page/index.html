Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any mea. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes

  • Preparation: 5 minutes

  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs

  • Salt, to taste

  • Pepper, to taste

  • 1 tablespoon of buffer or oil

  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs
